PROBLEM

Due to expansion, numerous bus routes have been recently added and many of those routes stop at the same bus stop. Riders want to know what the next arriving bus is and how much time they have to get to the bus stop. Simply rushing to the stop when you see a bus coming no longer works because it might not be the bus the rider is expecting.

SOLUTION

A modern transportation app that allows users to view in real time when buses are arriving and daparting from any given spot. As well as having notifications sent when a delay has been reported so the user isn't left guessing.

PROCESS

Over the course of developing Melted’s brand, I made sure to conduct and research how to create a unique and marketable app that people would use and enjoy.

COMPETITIVE  ANALYSIS

When I was researching the top public transportation apps Moovit and Citymapper were at the top of the list. These two across the board were the apps that many articles referenceed as being the best in the game. This making them the fiercest competitors to Busy Bus as well as the apps that I could gain the most information from. Auditing both Moovit and Citymapper, I analyzed what made each strong and weak.

VIEW ANALYSIS

USER SURVEYS

I deployed a survey to try and find out what audience was actually using the bus system, other types of transportation systems, why they chose a specific transportation and what made or deterred people from using certain transportation apps.

VIEW SURVEYS

USER PERSONAS

I created user personas based on the information gathered through the transportation survey that was deployed onto the internet, targeting people that used different transportation systems but mainly focusing on buses. This gave me insight into who my users would be.

ELLEN

FEMALE • 23 • STUDENT

Ellen has been around the Internet all of her life. As a college student has to use the bus system to get her to classes on different campuses or anything elseoff campus. It helps her save money but not time. The technology that she is used to is lacking for the transportation she uses each day.

JIM

MALE • 35 • Sales Broker

Jeff loves convience, anything that can save him time and money is a major plus for him and his busy life. He doesn't normally use the bus since he tends to live in the fast lane but in extreme situations when his phone is dead and can't reach an UBER he'll use one. However, everytime he's forced to use the bus he still struggles with the schedule.

USER STORIES

With these user personas in mind I then thought about the different types of users that would need to accomplish with a bus transportation app. I broke them down into three different categories of new users, returning users, and all users. Each having their own specific needs and wants when it comes to their transportation needs.

WIREFRAME SKETECHES

Thanks to the user flows giving insight into which pages were needed, I then began to roughly sketch out multiple wireframe ideas to get a sense of how the app should be laid out and how it would work for my intended users.

VIEW SKETCHES

MOCKUP

During my user tests I witnessed first hand what was and wasn’t working with my mockups. This knowledge helped me identify my pain points, like colors being too light and text being to thin, and allowed me to fix them. With my more refined mockups, I was then able to then move onto coding my screens to create a product that can be viewed online.

VIEW MOCKUP

Conclusion

Having created this application from scratch, it was important to base each function on what people needed to improve their lives, like real-time data from mapping, transit, and crowdsourcing. This allows users up to date information the moment it happens so that if an accident were to hold up traffic on route 3, they would know to take route 4. This helping users in the long run because it allows them to complete their end goals faster, to get from point A to B. People don’t have time and the prior knowledge to anticipate when there will be a delay in their routine so having the app warning you that there is a delay and offering you a better solution allows users a worry-free commute and a better experience.
